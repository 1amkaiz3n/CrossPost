Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

CrossPost is a self-hosted social media publishing platform. This privacy policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use our service.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts, we collect and store locally on your server:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Social media account information (name, profile picture, account ID)
  • Content you upload for publishing (videos, images, captions)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Authenticating your social media accounts
  • Publishing content to your connected social media platforms
  • Displaying upload history within your dashboard

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own server using JSON files. We do not send your data to any third-party servers except the social media platforms you explicitly authorize. No data is shared with CrossPost developers or any other party.

5. Data Retention

You have full control over your data. You can disconnect accounts and delete upload history directly from the dashboard at any time. All data is removed when you disconnect an account or delete your uploads.

6. Third-Party Services

CrossPost interacts with the following third-party services through their official APIs:

  • Google (YouTube)
  • Meta (Facebook, Instagram, Threads)
  • LinkedIn

Each service has its own privacy policy and terms of service. Please review them separately.

7. Security

Since CrossPost is self-hosted, security depends on your own server configuration. We recommend using HTTPS in production and keeping your server credentials secure. OAuth tokens are stored locally and are never exposed to unauthorized parties.

8. User Rights

As a self-hosted user, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access all stored data through the dashboard
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Disconnect accounts to remove associated tokens
  • Export data via the backup feature

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the administrator of this CrossPost instance.
